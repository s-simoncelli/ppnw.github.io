Getting to Bath

Getting to Bath is easy by air, rail, or bus.

Bristol Airport is 1 hr away by direct bus connection: https://www.airdecker.com/

London Heathrow is 2-2.5 hrs away and can be reached:

  • by rail: http://www.nationalrail.co.uk
  • by bus: http://www.nationalexpress.com
  • by combination: http://www.railair.com

Great Western Railway (the train company that runs between London Paddington and Bath) offers discounted travel to conference attendees.
More information can be found here:

https://www.gwr.com/your-journey/tickets-railcards-and-season-tickets/business-travel/conferences-and-accommodation
