Venue

The conference will be held in two venues:

On Tuesday, Wednesday, and Friday, the days activities will be held at the historic Bath Guildhall in the city centre:

http://visitbath.co.uk/things-to-do/guildhall-p24991

On Thursday, as part of a larger water-related event at the University of Bath, the events will be held on the university campus:

http://www.bath.ac.uk/travel-advice/

They are approximately 1.5 miles apart, and we recommend staying in the city centre as most events will be focused there, although budget options are available on campus (see accommodation page for more details)
